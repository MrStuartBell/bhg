How It Works

Brutally Honest Guides turn one focused conversation with you into a niche-crushing book funnel that feeds your calendar with the right prospects—without you wrestling with ghost-writers, tech setups, or "bestseller" vanity fluff.

1

Choose a Tight Niche

Pick the exact pocket of customers you want more of—"401(k) rollover refugees," "crypto Roth IRA die-hards," "new-construction dentists," whatever. The narrower the bullseye, the easier the win.

Benefit: You stop sounding like every other "financial planner" and start owning a problem real people are already Googling.

2

Nail the Title & Subtitle

On a quick call we stress-test angles, swipe the language prospects actually use, and lock a title + subtitle that grabs attention and telegraphs value in five seconds flat.

Benefit: Your book cover works like a mini-billboard in ads, feeds, and inboxes—no curiosity gaps, just "I need that."

3

Design the Call-to-Action

Before a word is written we agree on the single next step you want readers to take: book a call, start an assessment, join a webinar—whatever drives revenue fastest for you.

Benefit: Every chapter pushes toward measurable pipeline, not "gee-whiz" readership stats.

4

We Do the Deep-Dive Research

Our team pulls real search data, forum threads, and competitor blind spots to surface the 100+ questions prospects are already asking.

Benefit: Your book answers their burning questions before they hit back on the browser—instant authority.

5

Build the 35-Topic Blueprint → Refine to 30

We draft a 35-chapter outline, jump on a quick review call, swap anything you hate, add anything you love, and lock the final 30.

Benefit: Zero blank-page syndrome and a structure engineered for conversational flow.

6

Zero-Pressure Interview

You talk. We record. 90 minutes, tops. No "please write a chapter on Tuesday" homework. We extract your voice, stories, and hot takes while you sip coffee.

Benefit: One afternoon of your life = a ~150-page asset that sounds like you wrote every word.

7

Expert Editing & Content Amplification

Our editors merge your interview with the research, tighten the narrative, inject SEO-friendly phrasing, and add "Brutal Bottom Line" takeaways at the end of each chapter.

Benefit: Readers feel heard and guided; Google notices the substance.

8

Funnel Build-Out (Plan Dependent)

Book-Only
• Finished PDF + print-ready files
• DIY quick-launch guide
Essentials
• High-conversion landing page
• Checklist or assessment lead magnet
• Autoresponder that moves leads to your CTA
Everything
• Interactive quiz / scorecard to qualify leads
• Fortnightly value emails (done for you)
• Social posts to keep you visible
• Monthly strategy call to tweak & scale

Benefit: Choose the horsepower you need—nothing more, nothing less.

9

Launch & Iterate

Book-Only: follow the guide and fire when ready.
Essentials & Everything: we run a live launch call, hook up tracking, and fine-tune based on real-time data.

Benefit: Fast start, continuous improvement, and a funnel you can actually understand.

Why This Works

  • Authority Short-Cut: A real book in a niche beats generic blogs every day.
  • Conversation Magnet: Each chapter is a built-in email, DM, or social post prompt.
  • Ridiculously Low Time Ask: ~2 hours total on your calendar. We handle the rest.
  • Flexible Stack: Keep it DIY, hand off the tech, or let us run the whole engine.
  • Evergreen Asset: Unlike ads, your book keeps converting long after you've moved on.
